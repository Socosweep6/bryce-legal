These messages may include vendor communications, order inquiries, delivery scheduling, pricing discussions, and follow-ups related to product sourcing and business operations.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on active business communications. You will only receive messages relevant to ongoing business transactions or inquiries you have initiated.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out of receiving text messages at any time by texting STOP to the number from which you received a message. After sending STOP, you will receive a confirmation message and will no longer receive text messages from this program.

Support

For help or questions about this messaging program, text HELP to the number from which you received a message, or contact us directly by calling that number.

Carriers Supported

This program is available on all major U.S. wireless carriers.
