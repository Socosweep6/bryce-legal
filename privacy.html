Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Bryce Clausen ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you communicate with us via text message (SMS/MMS) or phone call.

Information We Collect

When you engage in business communications with us, we may collect your phone number, name, and the content of text messages exchanged for the purpose of coordinating business transactions, product sourcing, vendor communications, and related logistics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for business coordination purposes, including responding to inquiries, scheduling deliveries, discussing pricing and availability, and following up on business transactions. We do not use your information for marketing purposes unless you have explicitly opted in.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers (such as our messaging platform, Twilio) solely to facilitate message delivery.

Data Retention

We retain message records and contact information for the duration of our business relationship and as required for record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the number from which you received messages.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us by replying to any text message or calling the number from which you received a message.
